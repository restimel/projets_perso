Instruction
Goal

Tangram is a puzzle consisting of seven flat shapes, called tans.
The objective of the puzzle is to form a specific shape using all seven pieces, which may not overlap.

The silhouette which you should reproduce is given on the top right.
You can select a new shape to build by clicking on it.

Move Tans

  • To move a Tan, click on the piece and hold-on the button. Then move the piece where you want to place it. When you hold-off the button the tan will stay where you put it.
  • To rotate a Tan, click and hold-on the button on one of its corner. Then move your mouse to rotate the Tan to the orientation you want.
  • To flip a Tan, double-click on it. This action is useful only for the parallelogram Tan.

  • When moving a Tan, if you press on Ctrl, the Tan will not move over other Tans. This could help to avoid space between Tans.

Finish a shape

You have solved the puzzle when your Tans' shape is similar to the silhouette shape. The game will tell you that you have won by changing the background color.
If it is not the case try to move a little your tans to remove space between them or to avoid overlapping.

For many shapes there is not a unique solution. The game will validate your solution if it is close enough to the silhouette.

If you don't find the solution and you really want to see a solution, you can hit s to see Tans' shapes on the silhouette.
